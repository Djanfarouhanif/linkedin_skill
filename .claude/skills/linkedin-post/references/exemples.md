## Exemple 6 — Archétype : How-to / framework pédagogique

The best career advice I can give you this 2026:

(read this before you plan your year)

Stop setting dreams. Start setting goals.

Instead of writing down on a journal "Hit 100k in profit"
Follow my prove goal-setting framework that works.

Write down the goal: "Hit 100k in profit in 2026"
Write down the overview: "Launch 3 new offers"

This helps make it simple and achievable!

Now, we need to add urgency and mesurability:

Split the goal into tasks required to do every month.

January: i.e Finish the landing page, launch a waitlist
February: Post 4x pieces of promo content on Fridays
March: Launch new offer, schedule webinars and deliver.

This is how I've planned every launch I did this year.

I turned my "dreams" into small and actionable "goals".
Dont spend another year dreaming. DO it instead.
